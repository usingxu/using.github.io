Interactive Umberalla Stand


Living in the rainy Taipei, people often encounter the folowing situations: cannot find a place to put umbrellas on the messy stand, the folding umbrellas do not fit in the stand, or unable to find one's umbrella in a pile of wet umbrellas. Also, the shopkeepers are frustrated by the need to organize the stand space during rainy days.

To solve the troublesome problem, We designed a interactive system that encourage users to put their umbrellas in order by rewarding postive feedback based on "Fun Theory".

Role Taken


User Research

I participated heavily in user resarch, including both pre- and post-design research.

UI design

As the designer of the team, I designed the majority frames of the user interface starting from user work folow to prototype.

Visual Design

I designed the "Umbrella Monster", the mascot of the website.
