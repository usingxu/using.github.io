Soul Garden


In China, about 8 million people die every year, and most of the cemetery space will be depleted within 10 years. Government has encouraged ecological burial practices such as tree burial, however, the public’s burial choice does not change accordingly.

Soul Garden is a mobile APP that aims to encourage natural deep burials by changing people’s perception of death and reducing the sense of guilt and social pressures
