Community Triparties Service Network


In recent years, with the rise of the internet, the connection between real estate agency and the local community is deeply affected. However, real estate sell not only houses but also humanistic care, and the interpersonal interaction can never be replaced by cold machines.

To align with the "human-centric" core value of Xinyi Real Estate, we proposed a Triparties Service Network among Xinyi Real Estate, Community Committee, and Community Residentials.

Role Taken


Marketing Research

I participated in both pre-interview and the design of the viability quetionnaire.

Data Analysis

I used SPSS to analysis the quantitative data and produced a report of marketing viability.

Deliverable


Prize Won

The Best Proposals Award of 2019 "Sinyi Real Estate" College Creative Proposal Competition
